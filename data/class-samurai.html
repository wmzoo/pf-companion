Samurai

Ultimate Combat Class

Role

The samurai is a variant of the cavalier that trades mounted emphasis for a code of honor, resolve, and mastery of eastern weapons. They are disciplined melee fighters who challenge foes, fight with honor, and can draw on resolve to push through wounds and fear. They fill the same front-line role as a cavalier but with a more individual, less tactical flavor.

Alignment

Any.

Hit Died10
BABFull (1 per level)
Good SavesFortitude, Will
Poor SavesReflex
Skill Ranks4 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Bluff (Cha), Climb (Str), Craft (Int), Diplomacy (Cha), Handle Animal (Cha), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (history) (Int), Knowledge (local) (Int), Knowledge (nobility) (Int), Profession (Wis), Ride (Dex), Sense Motive (Wis), Swim (Str).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Samurai are proficient with all simple and martial weapons plus the katana, naginata, and wakizashi. They are proficient with all armor types and shields (except tower shields).

Class Features

Challenge (Ex)

Once per day per 3 samurai levels (minimum 1), a samurai can challenge a foe to combat as a swift action, gaining +1 damage per samurai level against that target. The samurai also gains a +2 dodge bonus to AC against the target of his challenge. The challenge ends when the target is dead, unconscious, or combat ends. This ability otherwise functions as the cavalier's challenge.

Mount (Ex)

A samurai gains the service of a loyal steed, as per the cavalier class feature.

Order (Ex)

At 1st level, a samurai must pledge himself to one of the following orders: Order of the Warrior (dedicate yourself to serving your lord and clan), Order of the Ronin (no master, self-reliant), Order of the Crane (elegance and etiquette), Order of the Dragon (service to allies), Order of the Flame (pursuit of glory in combat), Order of the Seal (protecting a secret or artifact), Order of the Star (serving a faith). Orders work identically to cavalier orders: granting edicts, order skills, and modifying the challenge ability.

Resolve (Ex)

Starting at 1st level, the samurai gains resolve that he can call upon to endure even the most devastating wounds and conditions. The samurai has a number of resolve points equal to 1 + 1/2 his samurai level. These are replenished after 8 hours of rest. The samurai can spend resolve to use the following abilities:

Determined (Ex): As a standard action, the samurai can spend 1 resolve to remove the fatigued, shaken, or sickened condition.

Resolute (Ex): Whenever the samurai is required to make a Fortitude or Will saving throw, he can spend 1 resolve as an immediate action to roll twice and take the better result. He must decide before rolling.

Unstoppable (Ex): When the samurai is brought to below 0 hit points but not killed, he can spend 1 resolve as an immediate action to act normally until the end of his next turn, after which his hit points drop to their actual (negative) value. Multiple uses extend the effect, each costing 1 resolve and granting 1 round of action.

Weapon Expertise (Ex)

At 3rd level, a samurai gains Exotic Weapon Proficiency and Weapon Focus as bonus feats, applying to one of the following: katana, naginata, or wakizashi.

Mounted Archer (Ex)

At 4th level, the samurai becomes skilled at firing ranged weapons while mounted. When on horseback and using a ranged weapon, the samurai reduces the penalty on ranged attacks by 2.

Banner (Ex)

At 5th level, the samurai's banner inspires allies. As per cavalier's banner ability — +2 morale on fear saves and +1 on charge attacks within 60 feet, increasing every 5 levels.

Bonus Feat

At 6th level and every 6 levels thereafter, the samurai gains a bonus combat feat.

Greater Resolve (Ex)

At 9th level, a samurai can spend resolve to shrug off the effects of some conditions. When the samurai is affected by any of the following conditions, he can spend 2 resolve as an immediate action to negate the condition: confused, frightened, nauseated, or panicked.

Honorable Stand (Ex)

At 11th level, a samurai can make an honorable stand once per day. For 1 minute, the samurai gains DR 5/— and a +2 morale bonus to his Strength and Constitution.

Demanding Challenge (Ex)

At 12th level, as per cavalier — the target of the samurai's challenge takes –2 AC against all attackers other than the samurai.

Greater Banner (Ex)

At 14th level, as per cavalier — allies within 60 feet immune to shaken, and +2 to frightened/panicked saves.

True Resolve (Ex)

At 17th level, the samurai can spend 2 resolve to ignore the dying condition for a number of rounds equal to his Constitution modifier. He can also spend all remaining resolve to stabilize at 0 hit points as an immediate action.

Last Stand (Ex)

At 20th level, once per day the samurai can declare a last stand. For 1 minute, he is immune to death effects, stunning, and paralysis, and gains DR 10/—. If he drops to 0 or fewer hit points during this time, he automatically becomes stable. After the last stand ends, he is exhausted.
