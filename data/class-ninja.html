Ninja

Ultimate Combat Class

Role

The ninja is a variant of the rogue that trades some skills and talents for ki-powered abilities and a focus on poison, stealth, and sudden lethality. They excel as ambush predators and infiltrators, using ki to enhance mobility and deliver devastating sneak attacks. The ninja is fully compatible with rogue archetypes and vice versa for multiclassing purposes.

Alignment

Any.

Hit Died8
BABMedium (¾ level)
Good SavesReflex, Will
Poor SavesFortitude
Skill Ranks8 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Acrobatics (Dex), Appraise (Int), Bluff (Cha), Climb (Str), Craft (Int), Diplomacy (Cha), Disable Device (Dex), Disguise (Cha), Escape Artist (Dex), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (local) (Int), Knowledge (nobility) (Int), Linguistics (Int), Perception (Wis), Perform (Cha), Profession (Wis), Sense Motive (Wis), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Stealth (Dex), Swim (Str), Use Magic Device (Cha).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Ninjas are proficient with all simple weapons, plus the kama, katana, kusarigama, nunchaku, sai, shortbow, short sword, shuriken, siangham, and wakizashi. They are proficient with light armor but not shields.

Class Features

Sneak Attack

This works exactly like the rogue ability of the same name. The ninja deals extra sneak attack damage of 1d6 at 1st level, increasing by 1d6 every two ninja levels. See the rogue entry for full details.

Poison Use (Ex)

At 1st level, a ninja is trained in the use of poison and can apply it to a weapon as a move action without risk of accidentally poisoning herself.

Ki Pool (Su)

At 2nd level, a ninja gains a pool of ki points equal to 1/2 her ninja level + her Charisma modifier. As long as she has at least 1 point in her ki pool, she treats any Acrobatics check made to jump as if she had a running start. By spending 1 ki point as a swift action, she can gain one of the following benefits for 1 round: +20 foot bonus to speed, +4 dodge bonus to AC, advantage on one Stealth check made as part of that action. The ninja can use ki to power her ninja tricks. Ki refreshes after 8 hours of rest.

Ninja Tricks

Starting at 2nd level, and every two levels thereafter, a ninja learns a trick that either enables her to use ki in new ways, grants a combat ability, or gives her a new skill use. Unless otherwise noted, a ninja cannot select an individual ninja trick more than once. Ninja tricks marked with an asterisk (*) add effects to sneak attacks. Notable tricks: Acrobatic Master, Bleeding Attack*, Blind Sight, Combat Trick, Deadly Range, Deflect Arrows, Flurry of Stars, Forgotten Trick, High Jumper, Invisible Blade, Ki Block*, Ki Charge, Light Steps, Ninjutsu, No Trace, Pressure Points*, Shadow Clone, Slow Metabolism, Smoke Bomb*, Sneak Attack (extra die), Stalker, Sudden Disguise, Unarmed Combat Mastery, Vanishing Trick, Void Blade*, Wall Climber, Whispering Campaign.

Advanced tricks (10th level+): Assassinate, Blinding Bomb, Calm the Inner Flame, Evasion, Ghost Step, Hideout, Improved Evasion, Master Disguise, Redirect Attack, Skill Mastery, Slippery Mind, Undetected Sabotage, Unusual Anatomy.

No Trace (Ex)

At 3rd level, a ninja learns to cover her tracks, leaving no trace of her passage. The DC to track a ninja using the Survival skill increases by +1 for every ninja level she has. In addition, her training gives her a +1 insight bonus on Disguise skill checks and on Stealth checks when she is not moving. These bonuses increase by +1 for every three levels beyond 3rd.

Uncanny Dodge (Ex)

At 4th level, a ninja gains uncanny dodge as per the rogue class feature.

Light Steps (Ex)

At 6th level, a ninja learns to move while barely touching the surface beneath her. As a full-round action, she can move up to twice her speed. During this movement, she can move across any surface (including water, clouds, or even thin air) and her movement does not provoke attacks of opportunity. She must end her movement on a solid surface or she falls.

Improved Uncanny Dodge (Ex)

At 8th level, a ninja gains improved uncanny dodge as per the rogue class feature.

Improved Poison Use (Ex)

At 8th level, a ninja can apply poison to a weapon as a swift action.

Master Tricks

At 10th level and every two levels thereafter, a ninja can select one of the master tricks listed above in place of a ninja trick.

Hidden Master (Su)

At 20th level, a ninja becomes a true master of her art. She can, as a standard action, cast greater invisibility on herself. This functions like the spell, but lasts until she chooses to end it. In addition, whenever the ninja sneak attacks a creature, that creature is affected as if by a targeted greater dispel magic unless the creature succeeds at a Will save (DC 20). This dispel attempt only affects spells and spell-like abilities affecting the target. The ninja can use this last ability once per day.
