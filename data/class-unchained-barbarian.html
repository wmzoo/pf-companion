Barbarian (Unchained)

Pathfinder Unchained

Role

The unchained barbarian is a revised version of the core barbarian with several key improvements. Most significantly, the unchained barbarian's rage no longer restricts skill use, and she gains powerful instincts that scale automatically. Rage powers are replaced with a streamlined system. This is generally the preferred version for new play.

Alignment

Any non-lawful.

Hit Died12
BABFull (1 per level)
Good SavesFortitude, Will
Poor SavesReflex
Skill Ranks4 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Acrobatics (Dex), Climb (Str), Craft (Int), Handle Animal (Cha), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (nature) (Int), Perception (Wis), Ride (Dex), Survival (Wis), Swim (Str).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Same as the core barbarian: all simple and martial weapons, light armor, medium armor, and shields (except tower shields).

Key Differences from Core Barbarian

The unchained barbarian replaces the core barbarian's rage powers with a cleaner set of class features. The rage itself is also less restrictive — while raging, the unchained barbarian can still use all skills and abilities normally, including Intelligence- and Charisma-based ones. The fatigue after rage still applies.

Class Features

Rage (Ex)

As the core barbarian, except: while raging, the unchained barbarian does not take a penalty to AC, does not lose access to Dexterity- or Intelligence-based skills, and can use any ability. She still gains the +4 morale bonus to Strength and Constitution, the +2 morale bonus to Will saves, and takes the –2 AC penalty. Rounds per day: 4 + Constitution modifier, +2 per level after 1st.

Fast Movement (Ex)

+10 feet to land speed while wearing light or medium armor or no armor and not heavily encumbered.

Danger Sense (Ex)

At 3rd level, a barbarian gains a +1 bonus on Reflex saves to avoid traps and a +1 dodge bonus to AC against attacks by traps. In addition, she gains a +1 bonus on Perception checks to avoid being surprised by a foe. These bonuses increase by +1 every three levels thereafter (6th, 9th, 12th, 15th, and 18th level). This bonus replaces trap sense from the core barbarian and also applies against ambushes and surprise rounds.

Uncanny Dodge (Ex)

At 2nd level, as core barbarian.

Improved Uncanny Dodge (Ex)

At 5th level, as core barbarian.

Instincts

At 1st level, an unchained barbarian selects a rage power from the list below (called instincts in some printings). She gains additional rage powers at 2nd level and every 2 levels thereafter. All the same core barbarian rage powers are available, as the unchained barbarian uses the same rage power list. She also gains access to several new powers available only to unchained barbarians.

Damage Reduction (Ex)

At 7th level, the unchained barbarian gains DR 1/—. This increases by 1 at 10th, 13th, 16th, and 19th levels (max DR 5/— at 19th). This functions identically to the core barbarian.

Greater Rage (Ex)

At 11th level, morale bonus to Strength and Constitution increases to +6, Will bonus increases to +3.

Indomitable Will (Ex)

At 14th level, while raging, gains +4 bonus on Will saves to resist enchantment spells.

Tireless Rage (Ex)

At 17th level, no longer fatigued after rage ends.

Mighty Rage (Ex)

At 20th level, morale bonus to Strength and Constitution increases to +8, Will bonus increases to +4.
