Shaman

ACG Hybrid Class (Oracle/Witch)

Role

Shamans are divine spellcasters who commune with spirits. Each day, a shaman can access a spirit that grants bonus spells and class features, and she permanently bonds with a spirit as well. They also gain hexes like a witch. Shamans are versatile divine casters who can adapt their spirit focus each day, making them flexible party support and utility characters.

Alignment

Any.

Hit Died8
BABMedium (¾ level)
Good SavesWill
Poor SavesFortitude, Reflex
Skill Ranks4 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Craft (Int), Diplomacy (Cha), Fly (Dex), Handle Animal (Cha), Heal (Wis), Knowledge (arcana) (Int), Knowledge (history) (Int), Knowledge (nature) (Int), Knowledge (planes) (Int), Knowledge (religion) (Int), Profession (Wis), Ride (Dex), Spellcraft (Int), Survival (Wis).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Shamans are proficient with all simple weapons, light armor, medium armor, and shields (except tower shields). Unlike most divine spellcasters, a shaman using medium armor does not incur spell failure for divine spells.

Spellcasting

A shaman casts divine spells drawn from the shaman spell list, which is similar to the cleric and druid lists. She must prepare spells ahead of time, gaining them through meditation and communing with her spirits. She must have a Wisdom score of at least 10 + spell level. DC is 10 + spell level + Wisdom modifier. She prepares spells after 8 hours of rest. Bonus spells are based on Wisdom. She receives one extra spell slot per level that she can fill with a spell from her wandering spirit's list.

Class Features

Spirit (Su)

A shaman forms a mystical bond with one spirit from the shaman spirit list. This is her spirit. She gains the spirit's spirit magic spells, spirit animal, and one spirit power. At 6th level, she gains the second spirit power. At 12th level, the third. At 20th level, the final power. Spirits include: Battle, Bones, Flame, Heavens, Life, Lore, Nature, Stone, Wandering Mind, Waves, Wind.

Example — Life Spirit: Spirit magic spells: cure light wounds (1st), cure moderate wounds (2nd), cure serious wounds (3rd), cure critical wounds (4th), breath of life (5th), heal (6th), regenerate (7th), mass cure critical wounds (8th), mass heal (9th). Powers: healing (1st, as witch hex), channel (6th, as cleric), life link (12th, as oracle), spirit of the dead (20th, raise dead as a standard action).

Spirit Animal (Ex)

At 1st level, a shaman's spirit guides her via a specific spirit animal. This animal serves as her familiar, using her shaman level as her effective wizard level. If the spirit animal is slain, the shaman must wait until the next day to re-commune with her spirit to replace it. The type of spirit animal is determined by the shaman's spirit choice.

Hex

A shaman learns a number of magic tricks called hexes. At 2nd level and every 2 levels thereafter, a shaman gains a new hex. A shaman cannot select an individual hex more than once unless noted. The DC is 10 + 1/2 the shaman's level + Wisdom modifier. She can choose from the following hexes: all witch hexes that are available to the witch at the time the shaman gains the hex level, and spirit hexes granted by her spirit.

Wandering Spirit (Su)

At 4th level, a shaman can form a temporary bond with a spirit other than her primary spirit. Each day when she prepares spells, she can select any one spirit (other than her primary spirit). She gains that spirit's spirit magic spells and first spirit power. She can use hex choices to gain the wandering spirit's hexes instead of normal hexes. At 12th level, she gains the wandering spirit's second power as well.

Spirit Ability (Su)

At 4th level and every 4 levels thereafter, the shaman gains an additional spirit ability from her primary spirit.

Manifestation (Su)

At 20th level, a shaman's connection to her spirit becomes so strong that it manifests physically. She gains a powerful ability depending on her primary spirit (each is unique and transformative).
