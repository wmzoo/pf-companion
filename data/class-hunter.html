Hunter

ACG Hybrid Class (Druid/Ranger)

Role

Hunters are nature warriors who work in concert with a powerful animal companion. They share the ranger's focus on terrain and favored enemies and the druid's wild shape and nature magic. Most uniquely, hunters can share teamwork feats with their animal companion automatically, making the hunter/companion pair a highly coordinated fighting unit.

Alignment

Any.

Hit Died8
BABMedium (¾ level)
Good SavesFortitude, Reflex
Poor SavesWill
Skill Ranks6 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Acrobatics (Dex), Climb (Str), Craft (Int), Handle Animal (Cha), Heal (Wis), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (dungeoneering) (Int), Knowledge (geography) (Int), Knowledge (nature) (Int), Perception (Wis), Profession (Wis), Ride (Dex), Spellcraft (Int), Stealth (Dex), Survival (Wis), Swim (Str).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Hunters are proficient with all simple and martial weapons, and with light and medium armor. They can cast hunter spells while wearing light or medium armor without arcane spell failure.

Spellcasting

A hunter casts divine spells drawn from the druid and ranger spell lists (combining them into one list). She can prepare and cast any spell on this list. She must have a Wisdom score of at least 10 + spell level. DC is 10 + spell level + Wisdom modifier. She prepares spells after 8 hours of rest. Bonus spells from high Wisdom.

Class Features

Animal Companion (Ex)

At 1st level, a hunter forms a bond with an animal companion. A hunter may begin play with any of the animals listed in the animal companions section. This animal is a loyal companion that accompanies the hunter on adventures. The hunter uses her hunter level as her effective druid level for determining the abilities of her animal companion. The hunter and her animal companion can use the same space and the animal companion does not provoke attacks of opportunity by moving through threatened squares.

Animal Focus (Su)

At 1st level, a hunter can take on the aspect of an animal as a swift action. She must select one type of animal to emulate, gaining a bonus or special ability based on the animal. The hunter can use this ability for a number of minutes per day equal to her level. This duration does not need to be consecutive, but must be spent in 1-minute increments. At 8th level, she can apply a different animal focus to her animal companion as well. At 12th level, she can maintain aspects while using wild shape. Animal focuses include: Bear (+2 enhancement bonus to Str), Bull (+2 enhancement bonus to Con), Falcon (+4 competence bonus to Perception), Frog (5-ft. increase to reach with tongue for grapple), Monkey (+4 competence bonus to Climb), Mouse (evasion), Owl (+4 competence bonus to Stealth), Snake (+2 competence bonus to CMB on grapple), Stag (+5 feet speed enhancement), Tiger (+2 enhancement bonus to Dex), Wolf (+4 competence bonus to Survival to track).

Wild Empathy (Ex)

A hunter can improve the attitude of an animal, as the ranger ability.

Teamwork Feat

At 3rd level, and every 3 levels thereafter, the hunter gains a bonus teamwork feat. The hunter must meet the prerequisites for this feat.

Precise Companion (Ex)

At 2nd level, a hunter chooses either Precise Strike or Outflank as a bonus feat. She doesn't need to meet the prerequisites. The hunter's animal companion automatically has this feat as well, as long as the hunter does.

Hunter's Bond (Ex)

At 4th level, the hunter can spend a move action to grant half her favored enemy or favored terrain bonus to her animal companion, functioning exactly as the ranger's hunter's bond ability.

Woodland Stride (Ex)

Starting at 5th level, a hunter can move through undergrowth at her normal speed without damage or impairment.

Improved Empathic Link (Su)

At 6th level, the hunter's empathic link with her animal companion becomes a full telepathic link with no range limit (as long as they are on the same plane).

Swift Tracker (Ex)

At 8th level, a hunter can move at full speed while tracking without penalty.

Raise Animal Companion (Sp)

At 7th level, once per day the hunter can cast raise dead, but only for her animal companion. This requires 8 hours and the normal material component.

Speak with Master (Ex)

At 9th level, the hunter and her animal companion can communicate verbally as if they had a common language.

Bonus Tricks

Starting at 10th level, the hunter's animal companion knows an additional trick per level beyond 10th (2 at 11th, 3 at 12th, etc.) These bonus tricks are in addition to those gained normally.

Speak with Animals of Its Kind (Ex)

At 11th level, the animal companion can communicate with animals of its own kind.

Wild Shape (Su)

At 17th level, a hunter gains the ability to use wild shape, as the druid ability, using her hunter level as her effective druid level. This ability can be used once per day at 17th level, with an additional use per day at 19th level.

Master Hunter (Ex)

At 20th level, the hunter becomes a true master of her prey. She can make a single attack against a favored enemy as a standard action. If the attack hits, the target must make a Fortitude save or die (DC 10 + 1/2 hunter level + Wis mod).
