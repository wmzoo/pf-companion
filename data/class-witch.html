Witch

APG Base Class

Role

Witches are arcane spellcasters who gain their magic from a mysterious patron through a familiar that serves as their spellbook. They specialize in hexes — powerful supernatural abilities usable at will or once per target per day — and fill roles as debuffers, support casters, and knowledge specialists. Their spell list blends cleric utility with wizard offense.

Alignment

Any.

Hit Died6
BABSlow (½ level)
Good SavesWill
Poor SavesFortitude, Reflex
Skill Ranks2 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Craft (Int), Fly (Dex), Heal (Wis), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (arcana) (Int), Knowledge (history) (Int), Knowledge (nature) (Int), Knowledge (planes) (Int), Profession (Wis), Spellcraft (Int), Use Magic Device (Cha).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Witches are proficient with all simple weapons. They are not proficient with any type of armor or shield. Armor interferes with somatic components and can cause arcane spell failure.

Spellcasting

A witch casts arcane spells drawn from the witch spell list. A witch must prepare her spells ahead of time, but unlike a wizard, her spells are stored in her familiar rather than a spellbook. The familiar must be within arm's reach during preparation. To cast a spell, the witch must have an Intelligence score of at least 10 + the spell level. The DC for saving throws is 10 + spell level + Intelligence modifier. A witch receives bonus spells per day for high Intelligence. A witch begins play knowing a number of spells equal to 3 + her Intelligence modifier (plus her patron spells). At each new level, she adds two new spells to her familiar. A witch can also add spells by finding scrolls and transferring them to her familiar (taking 1 hour per level and costing 50 gp per spell level).

Class Features

Patron Spells

At 1st level, when a witch gains her familiar, she must also select a patron. This patron is a vague and mysterious force, granting the witch power for reasons that may remain unknown. A patron adds spells to a witch's list of spells known at specific levels. These bonus spells are not stored in the familiar; they are part of the witch's innate knowledge. Patrons include: Agility, Ancestors, Animals, Deception, Elements, Endurance, Healing, Insanity, Light, Moon, Occult, Plague, Shadow, Strength, Transformation, Trickery, Water, Wisdom, and others.

Familiar

At 1st level, a witch forms a close bond with a familiar, which stores all her spells. The familiar improves as the witch gains levels. If the familiar is ever lost or dies, the witch can get a new familiar (at the cost of 1 week and 200 gp per witch level) but loses all spells stored in the old familiar and must recollect them. The familiar shares the witch's alignment and acts as a repository for her spells. Familiars: cat, rat, raven, toad, weasel, and others.

Hex

Witches learn a number of magic tricks, called hexes, that grant them powers or weaken foes. At 1st level, a witch gains one hex. At 2nd level, and every two levels thereafter, the witch gains an additional hex. Unless otherwise noted, using a hex is a standard action that does not provoke an attack of opportunity. A hex that causes a target to make a saving throw has a DC of 10 + 1/2 the witch's level + the witch's Intelligence modifier. Most hexes that cause a target to suffer a negative effect can only target a specific creature once per day. The witch cannot select an individual hex more than once unless noted.

Minor Hexes (available from 1st level): Cauldron, Cackle (free action to extend hex durations), Charm, Coven, Disguise, Evil Eye (–2 on one of: AC/attack/saves/ability/skill checks; Will negates; lasts 1 round, or forever on failed save), Flight, Fortune (reroll one d20, take better result; once/day per target), Healing (cure light wounds 1/day per target), Misfortune (reroll, take worse; Will negates; 1 round), Murksight, Prehensile Hair, Slumber (sleep, no HD limit; Will negates; shaking awake ends it), Speak in Dreams, Swamp Hag, Tongues, Ward, Water Lung.

Major Hexes (8th level+): Agony, Alphas Affliction, Beast of Ill Omen, Cooking, Death Curse, Eternal Slumber, Forced Reincarnation, Harrowing Curse, Hag's Eye, Ice Tomb, Infected Wounds, Retribution, Restless Slumber, Speak with Dead, Waxen Image.

Grand Hexes (18th level+): Death, Dire Prophecy, Lay to Rest, Life Giver, Natural Disaster.

Cantrips

Witches can prepare a number of cantrips each day. These spells are not expended when cast and may be used again. A witch can prepare a cantrip any number of times and it fills a cantrip slot, but does not consume it when cast.
