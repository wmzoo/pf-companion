Cavalier

APG Base Class

Role

Cavaliers are mounted warriors dedicated to a specific order that defines their code, bonus skills, and special abilities. They excel in mounted combat, leading allies with their tactician ability, and issuing challenges to single out powerful foes. They are durable front-liners with strong social skills tied to their order.

Alignment

Any.

Hit Died10
BABFull (1 per level)
Good SavesFortitude
Poor SavesReflex, Will
Skill Ranks4 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Bluff (Cha), Climb (Str), Craft (Int), Diplomacy (Cha), Handle Animal (Cha), Intimidate (Cha), Profession (Wis), Ride (Dex), Sense Motive (Wis), Swim (Str).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Cavaliers are proficient with all simple and martial weapons, with all types of armor (heavy, light, and medium), and with shields (except tower shields).

Class Features

Challenge (Ex)

Once per day, a cavalier can challenge a foe to combat. As a swift action, the cavalier chooses one target within sight to challenge. The cavalier's melee attacks deal extra damage equal to his cavalier level whenever he attacks the target of his challenge. The cavalier can use this ability once per day at 1st level, plus one additional time per day for every three levels beyond 1st, to a maximum of seven times per day at 19th level. Challenging a foe requires the cavalier to spend a move action to move toward the target each round. If the cavalier fails to move toward the target or attack the target each round, the challenge ends. The challenge remains in effect until the target is dead or unconscious or until the combat ends. Each cavalier's order modifies the challenge ability in different ways, as detailed in the order description.

Mount (Ex)

A cavalier gains the service of a loyal and trusty steed to carry him into battle. This mount functions as a druid's animal companion, using the cavalier's level as his effective druid level. The cavalier does not take an armor check penalty on Ride checks while riding his mount. The mount is always considered combat trained and begins play with Light Armor Proficiency as a bonus feat. A cavalier's mount does not gain the share spells special ability. A cavalier's bond with his mount is strong, with the rider detecting as a paladin for the purpose of spells and effects that affect paladins specifically.

Order (Ex)

At 1st level, a cavalier must pledge himself to a specific order. The order grants the cavalier a number of bonuses, class skills, and special abilities. In addition, each order includes a number of edicts that the cavalier must follow. If he violates these edicts, he loses the benefits of his order's challenge ability and resolve ability until he atones (via the atonement spell or similar magic). Orders include: Order of the Cockatrice (self-focused, intimidation), Order of the Dragon (teamwork, aid another), Order of the Lion (protecting a sovereign/leader), Order of the Shield (protecting commoners), Order of the Star (serving a faith), Order of the Sword (personal honor in combat).

Tactician (Ex)

At 1st level, a cavalier receives a teamwork feat as a bonus feat. He must meet the prerequisites for this feat. As a standard action, the cavalier can grant this feat to all allies within 30 feet who can see and hear him. Allies retain the use of this bonus feat for 3 rounds plus 1 round for every two levels the cavalier possesses. Allies do not need to meet the prerequisites of these bonus feats. The cavalier can use this ability once per day at 1st level, plus one additional time per day at 5th level and for every 5 levels thereafter.

Cavalier's Charge (Ex)

At 3rd level, a cavalier learns to make more accurate charge attacks while mounted. The cavalier receives a +4 bonus on melee attack rolls on a charge while mounted (instead of the normal +2). In addition, the cavalier does not suffer any penalty to his AC after making a charge attack while mounted.

Expert Trainer (Ex)

At 4th level, a cavalier learns to train mounts with speed and efficiency. The cavalier receives a bonus equal to 1/2 his cavalier level whenever he uses Handle Animal on an animal that serves as a mount. In addition, he can reduce the time needed to teach a mount a new trick or train a mount for a general purpose to 1 day per 1 week required. The cavalier can also train more than one mount at once, although each mount after the first adds 1 day to the total training time.

Banner (Ex)

At 5th level, a cavalier's banner becomes a symbol of inspiration to his allies and companions. As long as the cavalier's banner is clearly visible, all allies within 60 feet receive a +2 morale bonus on saving throws against fear and a +1 morale bonus on attack rolls made as part of a charge. At 10th level, and every five levels thereafter, these bonuses increase by +1. The banner must be at least Small or larger and must be carried or displayed by the cavalier or his mount to function.

Bonus Feat

At 6th level, and at every six levels thereafter, a cavalier gains a bonus feat in addition to those gained from normal advancement. These bonus feats must be selected from those listed as combat feats. The cavalier must meet the prerequisites of the selected bonus feat.

Greater Tactician (Ex)

At 9th level, the cavalier receives an additional teamwork feat as a bonus feat. He must meet the prerequisites for this feat. The cavalier can grant this feat to his allies using the tactician ability. Using the tactician ability is now a swift action.

Mighty Charge (Ex)

At 11th level, a cavalier learns to make devastating charge attacks while mounted. Double the threat range of any weapons wielded during a charge while mounted. This increase does not stack with other effects that increase the threat range of the weapon. In addition, the cavalier can make a free bull rush, disarm, sunder, or trip combat maneuver if his charge attack is successful. This free combat maneuver does not provoke an attack of opportunity.

Demanding Challenge (Ex)

At 12th level, whenever a cavalier declares a challenge, the target of the challenge takes a –2 penalty to its AC from attacks made by anyone other than the cavalier until the challenge is resolved.

Greater Banner (Ex)

At 14th level, the cavalier's banner becomes a rallying symbol that instills courage. Allies within 60 feet who can see the banner are immune to the shaken condition and receive a +2 morale bonus to saves against the frightened, panicked, and demoralized conditions. If such a condition is currently affecting an ally when the banner is raised, the condition is immediately removed.

Master Tactician (Ex)

At 17th level, the cavalier receives an additional teamwork feat as a bonus feat. He must meet the prerequisites for this feat. The cavalier can grant this feat to his allies using the tactician ability. Whenever the cavalier uses the tactician ability, he may grant any two teamwork feats he knows. He may grant one feat to some allies and another feat to others, as long as all choices are made at the time tactician is used.

Supreme Charge (Ex)

At 20th level, whenever the cavalier makes a charge attack while mounted, he deals double the normal amount of damage (or triple if using a lance). In addition, if the cavalier confirms a critical hit on a charge attack while mounted, the target is stunned for 1d4 rounds. A Will saving throw (DC 10 + the cavalier's level + the cavalier's Str modifier) negates the stunned effect.
