Gunslinger

APG Base Class

Role

Gunslingers are expert marksmen who combine deadly aim with supernatural focus called grit. They use firearms to devastating effect, and their deeds — powered by grit points — let them perform cinematic combat maneuvers. They are ranged damage dealers who can also serve as scouts and mobile skirmishers.

Alignment

Any.

Hit Died10
BABFull (1 per level)
Good SavesFortitude, Reflex
Poor SavesWill
Skill Ranks4 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Acrobatics (Dex), Bluff (Cha), Climb (Str), Craft (Int), Handle Animal (Cha), Heal (Wis), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (engineering) (Int), Knowledge (local) (Int), Perception (Wis), Profession (Wis), Ride (Dex), Sleight of Hand (Dex), Survival (Wis), Swim (Str).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Gunslingers are proficient with all simple and martial weapons, and with all firearms. They are proficient with light and medium armor, and with shields (except tower shields).

Class Features

Gunsmith

At 1st level, a gunslinger gains one of the following firearms: blunderbuss, musket, or pistol. Her starting firearm is battered, and only she knows how to use it properly. All other creatures treat her starting firearm as if it had the broken condition. If the weapon already has the broken condition, it does not work at all for anyone else. In addition, starting at 1st level, the gunslinger can restore a broken firearm to working condition with 1 hour of work using a gunsmithing kit. She can also craft bullets, pellets, and black powder for cost: 1 gp for 10 bullets or pellets, or 1 gp for 5 doses of black powder. She can also craft alchemical cartridges with a DC 20 Craft (alchemy) check.

Grit (Ex)

A gunslinger makes her mark upon the world with daring deeds. Some of these deeds are simple tricks learned in dusty back alleys, while others are astonishing martial techniques that few have mastered. Grit is a fluctuating measure of a gunslinger's ability to perform amazing actions in combat. At the start of each day, a gunslinger gains a number of grit points equal to her Wisdom modifier (minimum 1). Her grit goes up or down throughout the day, but cannot go above her Wisdom modifier (minimum 1), though some feats and magic items may affect this. A gunslinger spends grit to accomplish deeds and regains grit in the following ways:

Critical Hit with Firearm: Each time the gunslinger confirms a critical hit with a firearm attack, she regains 1 grit point. Confirming a critical hit on a helpless or unaware creature or a creature that has fewer Hit Dice than half the gunslinger's level does not restore grit.

Killing Blow with Firearm: When the gunslinger reduces a creature to 0 or fewer hit points with a firearm attack, she regains 1 grit point. Killing blow on helpless/unaware creatures or those with fewer HD than half her level does not restore grit.

Deeds (Ex)

Gunslingers spend grit points to accomplish deeds. Most deeds grant the gunslinger some momentary bonus or effect, but some provide ongoing bonuses. A gunslinger can only perform deeds of her level or lower. Unless otherwise noted, a deed can be performed multiple times per round.

1st level deeds: Deadeye (spend 1 grit — ignore all range penalties on one attack, or make a single grit-fueled shot to target touch AC at any range up to the gun's maximum range), Gunslinger's Dodge (immediate action, spend 1 grit — move 5 ft. and gain +2 dodge to AC against triggered attack, or drop prone for +4 dodge), Quick Clear (standard action, spend 1 grit — remove the broken condition from a misfired firearm), Gunslinger Initiative (spend 1 grit at the start of combat — gain a +2 bonus on initiative and can reload a firearm as part of the initiative check).

3rd level deeds: Pistol-Whip (standard action, spend 1 grit — make a firearm melee attack, dealing bludgeoning damage plus the firearm's misfire value; on hit, target may be knocked prone, Will DC 10 + ½ level + Wis negates), Utility Shot (spend 1 grit — use firearm to make a variety of trick shots: knock an unattended object off a target, make a warning shot, or shoot a lock).

7th level deeds: Dead Shot (full-round action, spend 1 grit — make all your firearm attacks this round as a single attack against one creature; roll all attacks, and the damage is combined into one damage roll), Startling Shot (spend 1 grit — shaken target for 1 round on a hit), Targeting (spend 1 grit — target a specific body part to cause an additional effect: head/blind for 1 round, torso/stagger for 1 round, arm/drop item, leg/become entangled).

11th level deeds: Bleeding Wound (spend 1 grit — deal 1 point of bleed damage per round on a hit, plus your Wisdom modifier, until treated with DC 15 Heal check or magical healing), Expert Loading (reload as a swift action once per round for free), Lightning Reload (reload as free action, spend 1 grit).

15th level deeds: Evasive (spend 1 grit — gain evasion for 1 round), Slinger's Luck (spend 1 grit — reroll any die roll you just made), Cheat Death (spend all remaining grit — do not die from one killing blow, instead drop to –1 hp; can only be used once per day).

19th level deeds: Stunning Shot (spend 2 grit — stun a target for 1d4 rounds on a hit, Fort DC 10 + ½ level + Wis negates), Dead Shot (improved version).

Gunslinger's Dodge (Ex)

See Deeds above. At 1st level this is a deed; at higher levels it improves automatically.

Nimble (Ex)

Starting at 2nd level, a gunslinger gains a +1 dodge bonus to AC while wearing light or no armor. This bonus increases by +1 for every four levels beyond 2nd level (to a maximum of +5 at 18th level).

Bonus Feats

At 4th level, and every four levels thereafter, a gunslinger gains a bonus feat selected from the following list: Rapid Reload, any combat feat that has Rapid Reload as a prerequisite, Improved Critical (firearms), Weapon Focus (firearms), Deadly Aim, Improved Initiative, Point-Blank Shot, Precise Shot, or any feat with Weapon Focus (firearms) as a prerequisite.

Gun Training (Ex)

Starting at 5th level, a gunslinger can select one specific type of firearm (pistol, musket, etc.). She gains a bonus equal to her Dexterity modifier on damage rolls when using that type of firearm. Furthermore, when she misfires with that type of firearm, the misfire value of that firearm increases by 2 instead of 4. Every four levels thereafter (9th, 13th, 17th), the gunslinger picks another type of firearm to apply this bonus to.

Gunslinger's True Grit (Ex)

At 20th level, a gunslinger picks two deeds that she has access to and that she could use by spending grit. She can use these two deeds for 1 grit point fewer (minimum 0) than usual. If the number of grit points to use a deed is reduced to 0, the gunslinger can use this deed for free.
