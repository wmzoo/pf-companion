Fighter

Core Class

Role

Fighters excel at combat — defeating their enemies, protecting their allies, and making a name for themselves through sheer martial prowess. While their repertoire of weapons and feats is wide, fighters are distinguished by their unparalleled mastery of combat itself, able to wield any weapon and wear any armor.

Alignment

Any.

Hit Died10
BABFull (1 per level)
Good SavesFortitude
Poor SavesReflex, Will
Skill Ranks2 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Climb (Str), Craft (Int), Handle Animal (Cha), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (dungeoneering) (Int), Knowledge (engineering) (Int), Profession (Wis), Ride (Dex), Survival (Wis), Swim (Str).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

A fighter is proficient with all simple and martial weapons and with all armor (heavy, light, and medium) and shields (including tower shields).

Class Features

Bonus Feats

At 1st level, and at every even level thereafter, a fighter gains a bonus feat in addition to those gained from normal advancement. These bonus feats must be selected from those listed as Combat Feats. Upon reaching 4th level, and every four levels thereafter (8th, 12th, 16th, and 20th), a fighter can choose to learn a new bonus feat in place of a bonus feat he has already learned. The old feat cannot be one that was used as a prerequisite for another feat, prestige class, or other ability. A fighter can choose metamagic feats as fighter bonus feats, but must have the ability to cast spells before taking them.

Bravery (Ex)

Starting at 2nd level, a fighter gains a +1 bonus on Will saves against fear. This bonus increases by +1 for every four levels beyond 2nd (to a maximum of +5 at 18th level).

Armor Training (Ex)

Starting at 3rd level, a fighter learns to be more maneuverable while wearing armor. Whenever he is wearing armor, he reduces the armor check penalty by 1 (minimum 0) and increases the maximum Dexterity bonus allowed by his armor by 1. Every four levels thereafter (7th, 11th, and 15th), these bonuses increase by +1 each, to a maximum –4 penalty reduction and +4 Dex bonus increase at 15th level. In addition, a fighter can move at his normal speed while wearing medium armor at 3rd level, and heavy armor at 7th level.

Weapon Training (Ex)

Starting at 5th level, a fighter selects one weapon group. He gains a +1 bonus on attack and damage rolls with weapons from that group. Every four levels thereafter (9th, 13th, 17th), he gains training in another group at +1, and all previous groups increase by +1. Maximum bonuses are +4/+3/+2/+1 at 17th level. Weapon groups: axes, blades (heavy), blades (light), bows, close, crossbows, double, firearms, flails, hammers, monk, natural, polearms, siege engines, spears, thrown.

Armor Mastery (Ex)

At 19th level, a fighter gains DR 5/— whenever he is wearing armor or using a shield.

Weapon Mastery (Ex)

At 20th level, a fighter chooses one weapon. Attacks with that weapon automatically confirm all critical threats and increase the damage multiplier by 1. The fighter cannot be disarmed while wielding that weapon type.
