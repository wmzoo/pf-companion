Bloodrager

ACG Hybrid Class (Barbarian/Sorcerer)

Role

Bloodragers combine the martial fury of a barbarian with the innate magic of a sorcerer. When they rage, their bloodline powers manifest alongside their physical ferocity. They are strong front-line melee fighters whose bloodline spellcasting sets them apart from standard rage-based classes. They are simpler to play than many casters but more flavorful than a straight barbarian.

Alignment

Any non-lawful.

Hit Died10
BABFull (1 per level)
Good SavesFortitude, Will
Poor SavesReflex
Skill Ranks4 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Acrobatics (Dex), Climb (Str), Craft (Int), Handle Animal (Cha), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (arcana) (Int), Knowledge (nature) (Int), Perception (Wis), Ride (Dex), Spellcraft (Int), Survival (Wis), Swim (Str).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Bloodragers are proficient with all simple and martial weapons, light armor, medium armor, and shields (except tower shields). A bloodrager can cast bloodrager spells while wearing light or medium armor without incurring arcane spell failure. Like any other arcane spellcaster, a bloodrager wearing heavy armor or using a shield incurs spell failure.

Spellcasting

Beginning at 4th level, a bloodrager gains the ability to cast a small number of arcane spells drawn from the bloodrager spell list. He must have a Charisma score of at least 10 + the spell level to cast. DC is 10 + spell level + Charisma modifier. He casts spells spontaneously (like a sorcerer) from his spells known. He receives bonus spells for high Charisma. A bloodrager cannot cast spells while raging unless the spell is a spell-like ability or he has the Still Spell metamagic feat.

Class Features

Bloodrage (Su)

The bloodrager's source of strength is his blood, whether tainted by the wrath of a demon or warmed by draconic fire. When a bloodrager enters a bloodrage, he temporarily accesses the power of his bloodline and gains a +4 morale bonus to Strength and Constitution, as well as a +2 morale bonus on Will saves. In addition, he takes a –2 penalty to AC. The increase to Constitution grants the bloodrager 2 hit points per Hit Die, but these disappear when the bloodrage ends and are not lost first like temporary hit points. While bloodraging, a bloodrager cannot use any Charisma-, Dexterity-, or Intelligence-based skills (except Acrobatics, Fly, Intimidate, and Ride) or any ability that requires patience or concentration. A bloodrager can bloodrage for a number of rounds per day equal to 4 + his Constitution modifier. At each level after 1st, he can bloodrage for 2 additional rounds.

Bloodline

At 1st level, a bloodrager must select a bloodline. This bloodline grants additional spells known, bonus feats, and bloodline powers that manifest during bloodrage. Bloodrager bloodlines are similar to sorcerer bloodlines but have different powers tailored for martial combat. Bloodlines include: Aberrant, Abyssal, Arcane, Celestial, Destined, Draconic, Elemental, Fey, Infernal, Undead.

Each bloodline grants: bonus spells added to spells known at specific levels, bonus feats chosen from the bloodline list at 7th, 13th, and 19th level, bloodline powers at 1st, 4th, 8th, 12th, 16th, and 20th level.

Example — Draconic Bloodline: Claws that deal 1d6 damage (1st), dragon resistances (4th), frightful presence (8th), breath weapon (12th), wings (16th), dragon form (20th). Bonus feats from the Draconic list. Bonus spells related to the chosen dragon type.

Fast Movement (Ex)

A bloodrager's land speed is faster than the norm for his race by +10 feet. This benefit applies only when wearing no armor, light armor, or medium armor, and not carrying a heavy load.

Uncanny Dodge (Ex)

At 2nd level, a bloodrager gains the ability to react to danger before his senses would normally allow him to. He cannot be caught flat-footed, nor does he lose his Dexterity bonus to AC if the attacker is invisible.

Blood Sanctuary (Su)

At 3rd level, due to the power of his blood, a bloodrager can stand confidently amid the effects of spells cast by himself or his allies. He gains a +2 bonus on saving throws against spells that he or his allies cast.

Bloodline Feat

At 6th level and every 6 levels thereafter, a bloodrager receives a bonus feat from his bloodline's feat list.

Improved Uncanny Dodge (Ex)

At 5th level, a bloodrager can no longer be flanked.

Damage Reduction (Ex)

At 7th level, a bloodrager gains DR 1/—. This increases by 1 at 10th, 13th, 16th, and 19th level.

Greater Bloodrage (Ex)

At 11th level, the morale bonus to Strength and Constitution during bloodrage increases to +6 and the Will save bonus increases to +3.

Indomitable Will (Ex)

At 14th level, while bloodraging, the bloodrager gains a +4 bonus on Will saves against enchantment spells.

Tireless Bloodrage (Ex)

At 17th level, the bloodrager no longer becomes fatigued at the end of his bloodrage.

Mighty Bloodrage (Ex)

At 20th level, the morale bonus to Strength and Constitution increases to +8 and the Will save bonus increases to +4. In addition, the bloodrager's damage reduction increases to DR 5/—.
