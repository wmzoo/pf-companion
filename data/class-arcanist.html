Arcanist

ACG Hybrid Class (Wizard/Sorcerer)

Role

Arcanists blend wizard preparation with sorcerer spontaneity. They prepare a small number of spells each morning like a wizard, then cast them spontaneously from that prepared list like a sorcerer. This gives them the flexibility of spontaneous casting while retaining control over which spells are available. Arcane exploits customize their magic significantly.

Alignment

Any.

Hit Died6
BABSlow (½ level)
Good SavesWill
Poor SavesFortitude, Reflex
Skill Ranks2 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Appraise (Int), Craft (Int), Fly (Dex), Knowledge (all) (Int), Linguistics (Int), Profession (Wis), Spellcraft (Int), Use Magic Device (Cha).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Arcanists are proficient with all simple weapons. They are not proficient with any type of armor or shield. Armor interferes with an arcanist's gestures, which can cause her spells with somatic components to fail.

Spellcasting

An arcanist casts arcane spells drawn from the sorcerer/wizard spell list. She prepares a number of spells each morning (like a wizard), but then casts them spontaneously throughout the day (like a sorcerer). She can prepare a number of spells equal to 4 + her Intelligence modifier at 1st level, with additional preparation slots as she gains levels. She can cast any prepared spell without expending the prepared copy — instead she expends spell slots. To prepare or cast a spell, an arcanist must have an Intelligence score of at least 10 + spell level. The DC is 10 + spell level + Intelligence modifier. Bonus spells are based on Intelligence. An arcanist begins with a spellbook containing all cantrips plus three 1st-level spells, and adds two new spells per level.

Class Features

Arcane Reservoir (Su)

An arcanist has an innate pool of magical energy that she can draw upon to fuel her arcanist exploits and enhance her spells. The arcanist's arcane reservoir can hold a maximum amount of magical energy equal to 3 + the arcanist's level. Each day, when preparing spells, the arcanist's arcane reservoir fills with raw magical energy, gaining a number of points equal to half her arcanist level (minimum 1). The reservoir can never hold more than the maximum amount. The arcanist can also regain points by draining magic items of their energy (a standard action that drains 1 charge and restores 1 point; this destroys the item if it reaches 0 charges). Points from the arcane reservoir are used to fuel exploits. Any points remaining at the start of the next day are lost.

Arcanist Exploits (Su)

By bending and altering the rules of magic, an arcanist learns to exploit gaps and exceptions in the laws of magic. Some of these exploits allow her to prepare more spells or affect how her spells function. Others are activated by spending points from her arcane reservoir. The DC for any saving throw against an arcanist exploit equals 10 + 1/2 the arcanist's level + the arcanist's Charisma modifier. An arcanist can learn a new exploit at 1st level and every 2 levels thereafter.

Consume Spells (Su): The arcanist can expend a prepared spell to add points to her arcane reservoir (1 point per spell level).

Counterspell (Su): By expending 1 point from her arcane reservoir as an immediate action, the arcanist can attempt to counter a spell as it is being cast. She must identify the spell being cast (Spellcraft DC 15 + level). She then expends a prepared spell of equal or higher level.

Dimensional Slide (Su): The arcanist can expend 1 point to teleport up to 10 feet per arcanist level as a move action. This counts as a 5-foot step.

Familiar (Ex): The arcanist gains a familiar, as the wizard's arcane bond ability.

Item Crafting (Ex): The arcanist gains a bonus item creation feat.

Metamagic Knowledge (Ex): The arcanist gains a metamagic feat as a bonus feat.

Potent Magic (Su): By expending 1 point, the arcanist can increase the caster level of a spell by 2, or the DC by 2.

Quick Study (Ex): The arcanist can prepare a spell from her spellbook as a full-round action, replacing a currently prepared spell. She must spend 1 point from her arcane reservoir.

School Understanding (Ex): The arcanist selects one wizard arcane school. She gains one of that school's powers (chosen when the exploit is selected).

Spell Resistance (Su): By expending 1 point, the arcanist gains SR equal to 5 + her level for 1 minute.

Greater exploits (11th level+) include: Bloodline Development, Burning Flame, Counter Drain, Energy Shield, Greater Metamagic Knowledge, Lingering Acid, Metamagic Mastery, Redirect Spell, Spell Thief, Swift Consume.

Consume Spells (Su)

At 1st level, an arcanist can expend an available arcanist spell slot as a move action, adding a number of points equal to the level of the spell slot expended to her arcane reservoir. She cannot consume cantrips (0-level spells) in this way. She can use this ability a number of times per day equal to her Charisma modifier (minimum 1).

Bonus Feat

At 5th level and every five levels thereafter, an arcanist gains a bonus feat, chosen from the same list as wizard bonus feats.
