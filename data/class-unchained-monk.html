Monk (Unchained)

Pathfinder Unchained

Role

The unchained monk fixes several mechanical issues with the core monk. It uses a new style strike system for powerful unarmed attacks, a ki powers system that replaces bonus feats, and full BAB at higher levels via the ki strike progression. The result is a more powerful and cohesive martial arts specialist. This is generally the preferred version for new play.

Alignment

Any lawful.

Hit Died10
BABFull (1 per level)
Good SavesFortitude, Reflex, Will
Skill Ranks4 + Int modifier per level

Key Differences from Core Monk

The unchained monk uses a full d10 hit die (up from d8) and full BAB. It replaces the confusing flurry of blows system with a cleaner version, bonus feats with ki powers, and adds style strikes as a distinctive feature. The ki pool is also better integrated.

Class Skills and Proficiencies

Same as the core monk. Same weapon and armor restrictions apply.

Class Features

Flurry of Blows (Ex)

At 1st level, the unchained monk can make a flurry of blows as a full-attack action. When making a flurry of blows, the monk can make one additional attack at his highest base attack bonus. This additional attack stacks with the bonus attacks from haste and other similar effects. When using this ability, the monk can make these attacks with any combination of his unarmed strike and weapons from the monk weapon group. For the purpose of these attacks, the monk's base attack bonus from his monk class levels is equal to his monk level. For all other purposes, such as qualifying for a feat or a prestige class, the monk uses his normal base attack bonus.

Unarmed Strike

As the core monk, but the damage progression is based on the monk's size and uses a more powerful die: 1d6 (1st–3rd), 1d8 (4th–7th), 1d10 (8th–11th), 2d6 (12th–15th), 2d8 (16th–19th), 2d10 (20th).

Stunning Fist (Ex)

As the core monk. Gained at 1st level as a bonus feat.

Evasion (Ex)

At 2nd level, as core monk.

Ki Pool (Su)

At 3rd level, the unchained monk gains a pool of ki points equal to 1/2 monk level + Wisdom modifier. By spending 1 ki point, the monk can do one of the following as a swift action: make one additional flurry of blows attack at his highest attack bonus, increase his speed by 20 feet for 1 round, or give himself a +4 dodge bonus to AC for 1 round. The ki pool refreshes after 8 hours of rest. The ki strike progression is the same as core monk (magic at 4th, cold iron/silver at 7th, lawful at 10th, adamantine at 16th).

Style Strike (Ex)

At 5th level, as part of a flurry of blows, the unchained monk can designate one of his attacks as a style strike. A style strike has an additional effect beyond normal damage, chosen from the style strike list. He can use this once per flurry at 5th level, twice at 11th, and three times at 17th level. A monk can choose the same style strike multiple times in a flurry.

Style strikes available as the monk gains levels include: Elbow Smash (5th, move opponent 5 ft.), Flying Kick (5th, move up to speed before the attack, no AoO), Hammer Blow (5th, knock prone, Reflex save negates), Spinning Throw (5th, bull rush without AoO), Leg Sweep (9th, trip attempt as free action), Shattering Punch (9th, sunder without AoO), Blinding Strike (13th, blinded 1 round, Fort negates), Tongue of the Sun and Moon (17th, target loses any immunity to critical hits for 1 round).

Ki Powers

At 4th level and every 2 levels thereafter, the unchained monk can select a ki power, which replaces the bonus feat system. Ki powers include supernatural abilities fueled by ki, as well as scaled versions of the monk's core abilities. Ki powers include: Acrobatic Steps, Barkskin, Blind-Fight, Combat Reflexes, Diamond Soul, Deflect Arrows, Elemental Fist, Empty Body, Enduring Scrutiny, Feather Step, Flying Kick, Improved Blind-Fight, Ki Metabolism, Ki Perception, Ki Stand, Ki Strike (various types), Meditative Concentration, Qinggong powers, Scorpion Style, Slow Fall (improved), Swift Foot, Wholeness of Body, and more.

Fast Movement (Ex)

+10 feet at 3rd, +20 at 6th, +30 at 9th, +40 at 12th, +50 at 15th, +60 at 18th. As core monk.

Purity of Body (Ex)

At 5th level, immunity to all diseases.

Maneuver Training (Ex)

At 3rd level, as core monk (uses monk level instead of BAB for CMB).

Still Mind (Ex)

At 3rd level, +2 bonus on saves vs. enchantments.

Slow Fall (Ex)

At 4th level, reduces fall damage as core monk.

High Jump (Ex)

At 5th level, as core monk.

Wholeness of Body (Su)

At 7th level (as a ki power option), heals 2 × monk level hp per day.

Improved Evasion (Ex)

At 9th level, as core monk.

Diamond Body (Su)

At 11th level, immunity to poisons.

Abundant Step (Su)

At 12th level, dimension door as a move action (2 ki).

Diamond Soul (Ex)

At 13th level, SR equal to monk level + 10.

Quivering Palm (Su)

At 15th level, as core monk.

Timeless Body (Ex)

At 17th level, no aging penalties.

Tongue of the Sun and Moon (Ex)

At 17th level, speak with any living creature.

Empty Body (Su)

At 19th level, ethereal state for 1 min per level (3 ki).

Perfect Self

At 20th level, becomes an outsider with DR 10/chaotic, as core monk.
