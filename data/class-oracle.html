Oracle

APG Base Class

Role

Oracles are divine spellcasters who cast spontaneously like sorcerers, but draw their power from a mystery — a theme of divine power that grants both boons (revelations) and a debilitating curse. They are highly versatile healers and battlefield controllers, and their mystery determines their flavor and capabilities.

Alignment

Any.

Hit Died8
BABMedium (¾ level)
Good SavesWill
Poor SavesFortitude, Reflex
Skill Ranks4 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Craft (Int), Diplomacy (Cha), Heal (Wis), Knowledge (history) (Int), Knowledge (planes) (Int), Knowledge (religion) (Int), Profession (Wis), Sense Motive (Wis), Spellcraft (Int).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Oracles are proficient with all simple weapons, light armor, medium armor, and shields (except tower shields). Unlike arcane spellcasters, oracles wearing armor do not incur spell failure for oracle spells.

Spellcasting

An oracle casts divine spells drawn from the cleric spell list plus any spells granted by her mystery. She can cast any spell she knows without preparing it ahead of time. To cast, the oracle needs a Charisma score of at least 10 + spell level. The DC is 10 + spell level + Charisma modifier. She gains bonus spells per day for high Charisma. Her spells known are limited and fixed per level, similar to a sorcerer. At 4th level and every two levels thereafter, she can swap one known spell for another of any level she can cast.

Class Features

Mystery

Each oracle draws power from a mystery that infuses her with divine power and shapes her abilities. She chooses a mystery at 1st level; this choice cannot be changed. Each mystery grants a class skill, additional spells added to spells known at specific levels, and a set of revelations the oracle can choose from. Mysteries include: Battle, Bones, Flame, Heavens, Life, Lore, Nature, Solar, Stone, Waves, Wind, Wood.

Life Mystery (common choice): Class skill: Heal. Added spells: detect undead (2nd), lesser restoration (4th), neutralize poison (6th), restoration (8th), breath of life (10th), heal (12th), greater restoration (14th), mass heal (16th), true resurrection (18th). Revelations: channel (as cleric), enhanced cures, healing hands, life link, lifegiving, lifesense, safe curing, spirit boost, delay affliction, spirit of the warrior, spirit walk, trance of three.

Battle Mystery: Class skill: Intimidate. Added spells: divine favor, spiritual weapon, magic vestment, spell immunity, righteous might, mass bull's strength, power word blind, power word stun, power word kill. Revelations: battlefield clarity, combat healer, iron skin, maneuver mastery, master of arms, misleading strike, resiliency, skill at arms, surprising charge, war sight, weapon mastery.

Oracle's Curse (Ex)

Each oracle is afflicted with a curse that shapes her abilities. This curse is determined at 1st level and cannot be changed. The oracle's curse does not solely disadvantage her — it also unlocks some of her mystery's revelations. Curses include: Clouded Vision (darkvision 60 ft., then blindsense 30 ft. at 5th, then blindsight 15 ft. at 10th; but never sees beyond 30 ft.), Deaf (deaf, but immune to sonic, casts cleric/oracle spells as though using Silent Spell for free, gains +3 on initiative), Haunted (unseen spirits steal things, retrieve stored items as a move action instead of free; but at 5th gain mage hand and ghost sound at will, at 10th levitate and minor image at will), Lame (speed reduced 10 ft., cannot run; but immune to fatigue at 5th, and at 10th immune to exhaustion), Tongues (during combat must speak in an ancient language, allies cannot understand; but gains one language per level), Wasting (–4 Cha for diplomacy, –4 Str for combat maneuvers; but gain +4 on saves vs disease and poison at 5th, immunity at 15th).

Orisons

Oracles learn a number of orisons (0-level spells) each day. These do not consume spell slots and may be reused.

Revelations

At 1st level, 3rd level, and every four levels thereafter (7th, 11th, 15th, 19th), an oracle uncovers a revelation chosen from her mystery's list. Unless otherwise noted, activating a revelation is a standard action. At 1st level she gains one revelation, plus additional revelations at the levels listed. Some revelations have prerequisites.

Final Revelation

At 20th level, an oracle learns the final revelation of her mystery, granting her a powerful ability related to her mystery's theme. For example, a Life mystery oracle becomes an avatar of life: death effects automatically fail, she gains fast healing 10, and when she dies she automatically casts true resurrection on herself once per day.
