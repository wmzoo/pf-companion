Brawler

ACG Hybrid Class (Fighter/Monk)

Role

Brawlers are unarmed combatants who combine the fighter's martial versatility with the monk's unarmed prowess. Unlike monks, they have no alignment restriction and no ki pool, but they gain martial flexibility — the ability to spontaneously learn combat feats for short durations. This makes them highly adaptable in combat without requiring extensive feat planning.

Alignment

Any.

Hit Died10
BABFull (1 per level)
Good SavesFortitude, Reflex
Poor SavesWill
Skill Ranks4 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Acrobatics (Dex), Climb (Str), Craft (Int), Escape Artist (Dex), Handle Animal (Cha), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (dungeoneering) (Int), Knowledge (local) (Int), Perception (Wis), Profession (Wis), Ride (Dex), Sense Motive (Wis), Swim (Str).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Brawlers are proficient with all simple weapons plus the handaxe, short sword, and weapons from the close fighter weapon group. They are proficient with light armor and medium armor, and with shields (except tower shields).

Class Features

Brawler's Cunning (Ex)

If the brawler's Intelligence score is less than 13, it counts as 13 for the purpose of meeting the prerequisites of combat feats.

Martial Flexibility (Ex)

A brawler can take a move action to gain the benefit of a combat feat she doesn't possess. This effect lasts for 1 minute. The brawler must otherwise meet all the feat's prerequisites. She may use this ability a number of times per day equal to 3 + 1/2 her brawler level (minimum 1). The brawler can use this ability again before the duration expires to replace the previous combat feat with another choice. If a combat feat has a daily use limitation (such as Stunning Fist), any uses of that combat feat while gained from this ability count toward that feat's daily limit. At 6th level, a brawler can use this ability to gain the benefit of two combat feats at the same time. She can also use a swift action to gain the benefit of one combat feat for 1 minute. At 12th level, she can use this ability to gain the benefit of three combat feats at the same time. She can also use a free action to gain the benefit of one combat feat for 1 minute. At 18th level, she can use this ability to gain the benefit of four combat feats at the same time.

Unarmed Strike

At 1st level, a brawler gains Improved Unarmed Strike as a bonus feat. A brawler may attack with fists, elbows, knees, and feet. The damage of a brawler's unarmed strike: 1d6 (1st–3rd), 1d8 (4th–7th), 1d10 (8th–11th), 2d6 (12th–15th), 2d8 (16th–19th), 2d10 (20th). The brawler can deal lethal or nonlethal damage with unarmed strikes at no penalty.

Bonus Combat Feat

At 2nd level and every 3 levels thereafter, a brawler gains a bonus combat feat in addition to those gained from normal advancement. These feats must be from the following list: any feat with "Improved" or "Greater" in its name that improves a combat maneuver, Catch Off-Guard, Combat Reflexes, Deflect Arrows, Dodge, Improved Grapple, Scorpion Style, Throw Anything, and any feat that lists Improved Unarmed Strike as a prerequisite. The brawler must otherwise meet the feat's prerequisites. At 6th level, she can also choose from feats from the Close Weapon Mastery list, and at 10th from the Brawler's Strike list.

Maneuver Training (Ex)

At 3rd level, a brawler can select one combat maneuver to receive additional training. She gains a +1 bonus on combat maneuver checks when performing that maneuver and a +1 bonus to her CMD when defending against that maneuver. At 7th level and every 4 levels thereafter, she gains an additional +1 bonus and can select a new maneuver (or the same one for a higher bonus) each time.

AC Bonus (Ex)

At 4th level, when a brawler wears no armor or light armor, she adds her Charisma bonus to her AC and CMD. This bonus increases by +1 at 9th and every 4 levels thereafter.

Brawler's Strike (Ex)

At 5th level, a brawler's unarmed strikes are treated as magic weapons for overcoming DR. At 9th level, as cold iron and silver. At 12th level, as a specific alignment (matching the brawler's own). At 17th level, as adamantine.

Knockout (Ex)

At 4th level, once per day, a brawler can unleash a devastating strike. She must announce this before making her attack roll. If she hits and the target is not immune to critical hits, the target must make a Fortitude save (DC 10 + 1/2 the brawler's level + the higher of the brawler's Strength or Dexterity modifier) or fall unconscious for 1d6 rounds. The brawler can use this ability twice per day at 10th level and three times per day at 16th level.

Close Weapon Mastery (Ex)

At 5th level, a brawler's damage with close weapons increases. When wielding a close weapon, she uses the brawler's unarmed strike damage table instead of the weapon's damage die, if that results in more damage.

Awesome Blow (Ex)

At 16th level, the brawler may as a standard action perform an awesome blow combat maneuver against a corporeal creature of her size or smaller. If the maneuver succeeds, the opponent takes damage as if from the brawler's unarmed strike, is knocked flying 10 feet in a direction of the brawler's choice, and falls prone. The target must make a Fortitude save (DC 10 + 1/2 the brawler's level + Str mod) or be stunned for 1d4 rounds. If a wall or barrier is in the path, the target takes 1d6 additional damage and stops.

Perfect Warrior

At 20th level, a brawler has reached the pinnacle of her art. She gains mastery over two combat feats she has already selected with martial flexibility, gaining their benefits at all times without spending uses of martial flexibility.
