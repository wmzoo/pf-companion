Sorcerer

Core Class

Role

Sorcerers are arcane spellcasters who draw on innate magic from a powerful bloodline. Unlike wizards, they cast spontaneously from a limited selection of spells known, but require no preparation. Their Charisma-based magic, bloodline powers, and flexible daily casting make them potent blasters and utility casters who adapt to any situation in the moment.

Alignment

Any.

Hit Died6
BABSlow (½ level)
Good SavesWill
Poor SavesFortitude, Reflex
Skill Ranks2 + Int modifier per level

Class Skills

Appraise (Int), Bluff (Cha), Craft (Int), Fly (Dex), Intimidate (Cha), Knowledge (arcana) (Int), Profession (Wis), Spellcraft (Int), Use Magic Device (Cha).

Weapon and Armor Proficiency

Sorcerers are proficient with all simple weapons. They are not proficient with any armor or shield. Armor interferes with somatic components and can cause spell failure.

Spellcasting

A sorcerer casts arcane spells drawn from the sorcerer/wizard spell list. She can cast any spell she knows without preparing it ahead of time. To cast a spell, a sorcerer must have a Charisma score of at least 10 + the spell level. The DC for saving throws is 10 + spell level + Charisma modifier. A sorcerer has a limited number of spells known (fixed by level) but can cast each known spell as many times per day as her spell slots allow. At 5th level and every three levels thereafter, she may swap one spell known for another of any level she can cast.

Spells Per Day / Spells Known

A sorcerer's spells per day and spells known are fixed progressions based on level. At 1st level she knows 4 cantrips and 2 first-level spells. She gains new spells known at each level per the table. Bonus spells per day are granted for high Charisma.

Class Features

Bloodline

Each sorcerer has a source of magic in her heritage granting spells, bonus feats, an additional class skill, and special abilities. A sorcerer picks one bloodline at 1st level; this choice is permanent. Bloodline powers are gained at 1st, 3rd, 9th, 15th, and 20th level. Bonus bloodline feats are chosen from a bloodline-specific list at 7th, 13th, and 19th level — prerequisites for these feats are waived. Bloodline spells are added to spells known at specific levels.

Aberrant: Class skill: Knowledge (dungeoneering). Bonus spells: enlarge person, see invisibility, tongues, black tentacles, feeblemind, veil, plane shift, mind blank, shapechange. Powers: acidic ray, long limbs, unusual anatomy, alien resistance, aberrant form.

Abyssal: Class skill: Knowledge (planes). Bonus spells: cause fear, bull's strength, rage, stoneskin, dismissal, transformation, greater teleport, unholy aura, summon monster IX. Powers: claws, demon resistances, strength of the abyss, added summonings, demonic might.

Arcane: Class skill: Any (player's choice at 1st level). Bonus spells: identify, invisibility, dispel magic, dimension door, overland flight, true seeing, greater teleport, power word stun, wish. Powers: arcane bond, metamagic adept, new arcana, school power, arcane apotheosis.

Celestial: Class skill: Heal. Bonus spells: bless, resist energy, magic circle against evil, remove curse, flame strike, greater dispel magic, banishment, sunburst, gate. Powers: heavenly fire, celestial resistances, wings of heaven, conviction, ascension.

Destined: Class skill: Knowledge (history). Bonus spells: alarm, blur, protection from energy, freedom of movement, break enchantment, mislead, spell turning, moment of prescience, foresight. Powers: touch of destiny, fated, it was meant to be, within reach, destiny realized.

Draconic (choose dragon type): Class skill: Perception. Bonus spells: mage armor, resist energy, fly, fear, spell resistance, form of the dragon I, form of the dragon II, form of the dragon III, wish. Powers: claws, dragon resistances, breath weapon, wings, power of wyrms. Dragon types and their energy: Black/acid, Blue/electricity, Green/acid, Red/fire, White/cold, Brass/fire, Bronze/electricity, Copper/acid, Gold/fire, Silver/cold.

Elemental (choose air/earth/fire/water): Class skill: Knowledge (planes). Bonus spells vary by element. Powers: elemental ray, elemental resistance, elemental blast, elemental movement, elemental body.

Fey: Class skill: Knowledge (nature). Bonus spells: entangle, hideous laughter, deep slumber, poison, tree stride, mislead, phase door, irresistible dance, shapechange. Powers: laughing touch, woodland stride, fleeting glance, fey magic, soul of the fey.

Infernal: Class skill: Diplomacy. Bonus spells: protection from good, scorching ray, suggestion, charm monster, dominate person, planar binding, greater teleport, power word stun, meteor swarm. Powers: corrupting touch, infernal resistances, hellfire, on dark wings, power of the pit.

Undead: Class skill: Knowledge (religion). Bonus spells: chill touch, false life, animate dead, enervation, waves of fatigue, undeath to death, finger of death, horrid wilting, energy drain. Powers: grave touch, death's gift, grasp of the dead, incorporeal form, one of us.

Cantrips

Sorcerers learn a number of cantrips (0-level spells) as noted on the spells known table. Cantrips are cast like any other spell but do not consume spell slots and may be used again.

Eschew Materials

A sorcerer gains Eschew Materials as a bonus feat at 1st level.
